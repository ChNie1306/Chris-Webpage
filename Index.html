Diamond Art Paintings are sheets of parchment that have a very sticky surface.
To complete the paintings, you have to place tiny gems on the sticky surface in
their assaigned places. For example, a blue gem will have a certain image,
number or symbol it is assigned to and will need to be places on all of the
spaces with said image on it.



For example, on this painting, the gems labeled "11"
will be placed on squares labeled "J."
